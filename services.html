Mapping & Surveying

Leverage the power of drone technology for precise mapping and surveying. Our services provide accurate orthomosaic maps, 3D models, and topographic data essential for construction, land management, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.

We utilize state-of-the-art drones equipped with high-resolution cameras and GPS technology to capture detailed aerial data. This data is then processed using advanced photogrammetry software to generate actionable insights and deliverables tailored to your project requirements.

Key Features:

High Accuracy Data

Achieve survey-grade accuracy for reliable measurements and analysis.

3D Modeling

Create detailed 3D models of sites, structures, and terrains.

Topographic Surveys

Generate contour maps and digital elevation models (DEMs).

Inspections

Our drone inspection services provide safe, efficient, and cost-effective solutions for examining infrastructure, buildings, and industrial assets. With specialized drones equipped with high-resolution cameras, thermal imaging, and zoom capabilities, we can access difficult or dangerous areas without scaffolding, rope access, or heavy machinery.

We help identify structural issues, damage, maintenance needs, and potential hazards while keeping personnel safely on the ground. Our detailed inspection reports include high-resolution imagery, thermal data, and professional analysis to help you make informed maintenance and repair decisions.

Inspection Applications

Building Inspections

Examine roofs, facades, gutters, and building envelopes for damage, leaks, or deterioration.

Industrial Inspections

Inspect towers, tanks, flare stacks, and industrial equipment for maintenance and safety compliance.

Solar Farm Inspections

Identify underperforming solar panels, hotspots, and maintenance issues with thermal imaging.

Utility Inspections

Survey transmission lines, substations, and utility infrastructure for damage and vegetation encroachment.
